ABOUT COZY KITCHEN

The cozy kitchen was set up by a few more Asian students who used to study at Warwick. In Asian culture, there is nothing like a steaming cup of soymilk or a bowl of congee to wake you up for the day. We wanted to not only give our Asian students a home away from home, but also to introduce students from all over the world to the cuisine of Asia, and to introduce them to the cuisine of the Orient!

We will be serving freshly squeezed soy milk for breakfast as well as meat and vegetarian porridge. Simple bean paste buns and milky buns will be provided on top of the food. For lunch and dinner we will provide 2 meat and 1 veggie boxed lunches that are heated and ready to eat depending on the ingredients of the day. There is no need for extra waiting.

"Food is culture, and we need to listen to it."

Opening hours: Monday-Friday from 8:30am to 7pm.

Address:1st Floor,Warwick Art Center
